About the CCA Conference

Welcome to the Climate Change Awareness Conference 20205(CCAC 2025) - to be held 28 May, 2025 in Rockfeller Center in New York, USA. The event is organized by Global Nest - an international association of scientists, technologists, engineers and other interested groups involved in all scientific and technological aspects of the environment, as well as in the application of methods aimed at sustainable development.

The conference will feature speakers representing both academia and industry who are experts in Climatology and the environmental issues. Thus, CCAC 2025 unites diverse researches and case studies from all over the world. It also offers many networking and publishing opportunities.

Opening Speech

Emma Smith was nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize this year. She held her first single picket last August at the Swedish parliament. As a special guest of CCAC, she will open our conference and tell about the impact of eco activism.

10:40AM - 2:30PM

Luise Gibson

Time to make real decisions

The next 2-3 years are a decisive period during which a whole series of political and investment decisions will be made that will determine the future for the next 10-15 years.

2:30PM - 3:15PM

Gabriel Khu

The issue of climate refugees

Every year, 20 million people are forced to leave their homes due to climate disasters. The threat of such relocations is primarily in poor countries. Climate crisis kills people, forces families to leave their homes.

3:30PM - 5:30PM

Calvin Tisson

Pros and cons of the winD energy

Wind energy is not a constant. With a traditional power plant, you can maintain a constant power output, but the amount of wind power depends on weather conditions. This means that you cannot fully rely on the wind.
